Theme park verdict

Current working view after the Universal Beijing question.

Universal Beijing: is Jurassic the only draw?

No — but Jurassic World Adventure is the clearest unique headline. The park is best if Beijing itself is part of the trip, not if you’re flying there only for one ride.

  • Also worth noting: Transformers Metrobase + Decepticoaster, Harry Potter, Kung Fu Panda indoor land, WaterWorld.
  • Current leaning: keep Beijing if Forbidden City + Great Wall are exciting too.

Park priority ranking

  1. Shanghai DisneylandLocked: Zootopia, Pirates, TRON make this a must-do.
  2. Tokyo DisneySeaMost unique Japan park; Fantasy Springs is the big current draw.
  3. Universal Studios JapanWorth it if Nintendo/Donkey Kong is a priority.
  4. Universal Studios BeijingGood with Beijing sightseeing; weaker as a standalone reason.

Open decisions

The bits to lock next as the plan gets sharper.

1. Keep Beijing?

Decision hinges on whether Forbidden City + Great Wall + Peking duck make Beijing worth the extra flight/train logistics, not just Universal.

2. Japan park choice

Pick one max: Tokyo DisneySea for uniqueness, or USJ for Nintendo. Skip both if you want a more cultural Japan week.

3. Trip dates

Dates will affect Chinese holidays, Japan crowds, park opening hours, weather, and whether Express/Premier Access is sensible.

Logistics checklist

Not final advice yet — just the high-impact planning reminders.

China setup

  • Check visa / visa-free transit eligibility before booking.
  • Set up Alipay and/or WeChat Pay with an international card.
  • Use Apple Maps, Amap/Gaode or Baidu; Google Maps is unreliable in mainland China.
  • Book major attractions with passport details where required.

Japan setup

  • Add Suica/PASMO/ICOCA to Apple Wallet if supported.
  • JR Pass likely not worth it for simple Tokyo → Kyoto/Osaka routing.
  • Book teamLab, Shibuya Sky, DisneySea/USJ ahead if chosen.
  • Use luggage forwarding between Tokyo and Kyoto/Osaka.
